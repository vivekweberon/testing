    Launch Campaign:

    • Create a Landing page for your campaign on your Website.
    • Prepare a list of target prospects with names and mailing address.
    • Create your Campaign by uploading your prospects and specifying your campaign landing page.

    Send Mail:

    • Generate personalized letter with handwritten address on envelopes.
    • Print, stuff, affix stamp and mail.

    Capture Interest:

    • Interested prospects who SCAN QR code are taken to your landing page.
    • Get to know the list of prospects with Names & Addresses who 'Raised their hands'.

    Engage & Convert:

    • Re-Target those prospects who showed interest and improve ROI.
      • By sending them a follow-up personalized mailer OR
      • Procure prospects' phone number from a 3rd party source and Text or Call them to follow up.
